Dr. Álvaro Tejeda Lorente

Chief Executive Officer and co-founder

Álvaro earned his PHD in Computer Science at the University of Granada working on the development of recommender systems and fuzzy linguistic modeling, before pursuing a career in telecommunications working for Telefonica as a lead data scientist. In 2020 he decided to put his experience into the development and application of deep learning and artificial intelligence in the financial and currency markets founding Veleta. AI. He continues to lecture for UNIR for the MSC courses in Artificial intelligence and Big Data as well as his research.

Marcos López

Chief Technology Officer and co-founder

Marcos is a Telecommunications Engineer by the University of Málaga with an extensive experience as Technical Team Lead. Over the last ten years, he has mastered the latest tools and technologies to manage any project.

Dr. Juan Bernabé Moreno

Chief Data and Research Officer and Co-Founder

Juan earned his PHD at the University of Granada in Computer Science along with his Masters in Soft Computing and Intelligent systems. Juan is a highly respected and published researcher alongside a hands-on approach to driving technology development, advanced analytics/AI and most recently quantum computing. Following his educational career he has pursued a career first as an IT consultant before advancing into the development of AI and Advanced Analytics in Telefonica and most recently with E.ON as the Chief Data Officer and Global Head of Advanced Data and Analytics.
Alongside his commercial pursuits he is visiting research fellow with the University of Oxford and has recently published a book - Energy Systems in Smart Cities and Rural areas.

Matthew A Timms

Chief Commercial Officer and Co-Founder

Matthew graduated from Southampton University with a degree in Physics before pursuing a career in commercial leadership of digital businesses and leading large corporates through significant digital transformation programmes.
Matthew most recently joined E.ON SE as the Chief Digital Officer in August 2016 leading the digital transformation programme for the E.ON group. Over the next years he built a holistic digital programme touching all areas of the business, including culture, digital re-platforming businesses, IOT, applying AI to wind turbines, grids and customer acquisition / retention.
In 2018, Matthew became responsible for the global digital, data and technology/IT organisation, an organisation comprising over 3,000 people and responsible for over €1.5bn in spend. Building the culture, capabilities and technology transformation to support the delivery of E.ON ambitions to digitalise the energy world for a sustainable and greener future.
Previously he grew the digital transformation, online sales and GTM capability at SAP. Before this he spent over 8 years responsible for commercial P&L leadership of digital business units growing the business at Santander and the Lloyds Banking Group, where he was responsible for a number of industry firsts and innovations.
Matthew has had over 20 years of building innovative digital growth businesses, innovation and capabilities across several industries including software, automotive, pharmaceuticals and financial services. With a strong digital and business leadership background, he has experience in building high growth businesses within large corporations.

Raúl Vicente Ferrer

Data Scientist

Raúl is Civil Engineer with a Master in Computer Aplications by the Open University of Catalonia. Raúl is alternating his work on the application of Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence at Veleta.ai with his passion of becoming and expert in Data Science, currently he is a PhD. candidate with focus on applying Deep Learning to solve problems in Smart Cities.

Isaac Triguero

Colaborator and Researcher

Isaac is an Associate Professor in Data Science at the University of Nottingham and is one of the most important Machine Learning researcher of the current time. Isaac is collaborating with Veleta.ai to help us to face any existing forecasting challenge as well as to find any other we could never think of.
